About Cosmos

Cosmos is an independent travel and tour operator providing a range of services including package holidays to the Tanzanian market and beyond.

We are dedicated to service excellence, providing undivided attention to our clients, meticulous attention to detail as well as uncompromising safety standards.

We have specialized and competent team emphasizes the importance of individual motivation. The employees of the group have earned international respect and recognition for their creativity and readiness and for their commitment to communication, hospitality and professionalism. They identify the client needs and creatively and efficiently co-ordinate their activities.

Our Cosmos Tours and Travel administration team is composed of a group enthusiastic and dynamic professional member each dedicated to our motto of “Attention to Details” Keeping our vision, “value for money & client satisfaction” as a compass.
